\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Interface for managing physics-\/related operations in the application. 

The \doxylink{classEngine_1_1Module_1_1IPhysic}{IPhysic} interface provides a method to apply physical forces and update entities\textquotesingle{} states based on physics calculations.
