\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Interface for audio management in the application. 

The \doxylink{classEngine_1_1Module_1_1IAudio}{IAudio} interface provides methods for handling sound effects (SFX) and music playback, including controls for volume, playback, pause, and stop.
